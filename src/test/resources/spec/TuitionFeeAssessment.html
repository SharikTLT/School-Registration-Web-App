Story 2: Tuition Fee Assessment

When a student submits enlisted sections, the system will provide the student with a computation of how much tuition the student has to pay.

Tuition is ₱1,000 per unit, plus and additional miscellaneous fee of ₱2,000.

Student must have a minimum load of 15 units and maximum load of 24 units.
